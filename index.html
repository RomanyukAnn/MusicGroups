Radioherd

Radiohead is a rock band from the UK. They started in 1985. Their music mixes rock, electronic sounds, and experimental ideas. Their albums OK Computer and Kid A are very famous and changed modern music. The singer is Thom Yorke. Their songs often talk about feelings, technology, and problems in the world.

Adrianne Lenker

Adrianne Lenker is the singer of the band Big Thief and also makes solo music. Her style is soft, calm indie folk. She writes very personal and poetic lyrics. Her songs often talk about love, family, nature, and pain. Her voice is gentle and emotional.

Chloe · Adrianne Lenker - Anything (Live from Greenwich Village with Nick Hakim)

Alex G

Alex G is an American indie musician. He started by recording music at home and sharing it online. His music changes a lot — some songs are soft and simple, others are loud or strange. He mixes different styles like folk, rock, and electronic. His lyrics are sometimes hard to understand, but very creative.
